Research

I'm interested in Fluid Mechanics, CFD, Machine Learning, Optimization, and Dynamical System. Much of my previous study is about Aerospace industry. Previous related projects are as below.

Numerical Investigation of supersonic airflow mixing and combustion scale effects
Weilong Chen, Jianhui Du, Guoyan Zhao, Fan Li, Mingbo Sun, Guangwei Ma, Fei Li, Taiyu Wang
China National Symposium on Combustion, Sep 2022   (Wall Paper)
conference page / graduation thesis

Establish mathematical models for the key sub-processes of fuel mixing and flame combustion in supersonic airflow, clarify the spatial scale effects of fuel injection and mixing, and the time scale effects of supersonic combustion and flame stabilization

GPU-accelerated computational methods using Python and CUDA
Weilong Chen, Panagiotis Moraitis, Johannes hansson   (Supervisor: Lars Davidson)
Report, 2022

Implemented CFD algorithms using the Cupy library in Python. Results showed that using a GPU with Cupy was able to significantly improve the computation time for the CFD algorithms tested. In some cases, the use of a GPU resulted in a speedup of over 110x compared to using a CPU alone. Investigations also dive into different GPUs, matrix sizes and block sizes.
